# Fishy Zombie Survival

Welcome to a zombie survival world where all the guns are fish and the knife is a fish too.

## Fish Weapons

1. **Blunderfin**
   - A slow but powerful trout-shaped blaster.
   - Fires sticky salmon rounds that stick to undead targets and explode.
   - Great for crowd control and heavy damage.

2. **Puffer Pistol**
   - A compact pufferfish handgun.
   - Shoots inflated water bullets that stun zombies and slow them.
   - Perfect for quick escapes and precision shots.

3. **Barracuda Rifle**
   - A long, sleek barracuda rifle with a sharp, fin-like barrel.
   - Fires high-speed needle rounds that pierce armor.
   - Best used for mid-range zombie takedowns.

4. **Angler Shotgun**
   - A bulky anglerfish shotgun with glowing lure sights.
   - Releases a spread of glowing shrapnel fish that home in on nearby zombies.
   - Excellent for close-range defense.

5. **Torpedo Tuna**
   - A heavy tuna launcher that fires explosive fish torpedoes.
   - Deals massive area damage and can clear groups of undead.
   - Use carefully because it has slow reload time.

## Fish Knife

- **Bladefish**
  - A deadly knife shaped like a sharp, quicksilver fish.
  - Fast melee weapon with a clean slash and a chance to bleed zombies.
  - Ideal for quiet stealth kills and conserving fish ammo.

## Survival Concept

- Survive in a ruined seaside town where radioactive fish have become your weapons.
- Collect fish ammo, craft upgrades, and explore the flooded streets.
- Use the fish guns and Bladefish knife to fend off zombie hordes and stay alive.
